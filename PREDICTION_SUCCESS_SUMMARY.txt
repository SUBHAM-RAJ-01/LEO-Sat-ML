================================================================================
                            PREDICTION PROCESS
================================================================================

Step 1: History Collection
  → Collect last 50 timesteps of link metrics
  → 7 features: queue, utilization, bandwidth, drops, delay, arrival, success

Step 2: Prediction
  → GRU model predicts next 10 timesteps
  → Input: (50, 7) array
  → Output: (10,) predictions

Step 3: Routing Decision
  → Use predictions to compute path costs
  → Avoid links with predicted high congestion
  → Result: Smarter, proactive routing!
